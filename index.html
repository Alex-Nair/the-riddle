The Riddle

I've seen plenty of ARGs online that have already been solved, and have always wanted to try and solve one myself. However, the sheer length of them or difficulty of most ARGs (since they're meant to be solved by many people, not just one person) has always turned me away from them. Hence, The Riddle.

The Riddle is a non-serious "ARG"-like puzzle that exists to give you the feel of deciphering the solution and uncovering a hidden message without needing to spend hours on a single riddle or puzzle. It's also a fun little project for me to make in order to learn CSS.

Now, The Riddle isn't meant to be a cakewalk. You probably will get stuck and have an urge to cheat or search up the solutions. Do so. I've even created a guidebook that you can reference for hints and full solutions, as well as where those codes or solutions came from. Play The Riddle in whatever way you'd like to, but I do believe that you should at least give everything a shot first before looking it up. You only get to experience a puzzle once, after all.

Anyway, enough chat. The button below will start The Riddle in full, and drop you into the middle of it. Good luck, and have fun.
